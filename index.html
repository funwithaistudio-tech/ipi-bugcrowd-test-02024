Maple Glazed Salmon with Roasted Vegetables

A simple weeknight dinner ready in 30 minutes. Serves 4.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Whisk together maple syrup, soy sauce, and olive oil.
  3. Place salmon and vegetables on a baking sheet, brush with glaze.
  4. Bake for 18-20 minutes until salmon is cooked through.

Nutrition info: 320 calories per serving, 28g protein, 12g fat.
