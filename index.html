Maple Glazed Salmon with Roasted Vegetables

A quick dinner that serves 4 and takes about 30 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers)

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 400F (about 200C).
  2. Mix the maple syrup, soy sauce, and olive oil to make a glaze.
  3. Place the salmon and vegetables on a baking sheet and brush them with the glaze.
  4. Bake for 18-20 minutes until the salmon is fully cooked.

Nutrition (per serving)

About 320 calories, 28g protein, 12g fat
